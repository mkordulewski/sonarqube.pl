A new way of measuring understandability

Thomas J. Mc Cabe introduced Cyclomatic Complexity in 1976 as a way to guide programmers in writing methods that "are both testable and maintainable". At SonarSource, we believe Cyclomatic Complexity works very well for measuring testability, but not maintainability.
